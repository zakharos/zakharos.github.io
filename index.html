Sergey Zakharov

I am a senior machine learning research scientist at Toyota Research Institute and a former PhD candidate at the Chair for Computer Aided Medical Procedures, Technical University of Munich and Siemens AG. My research focuses on scene understanding and covers such topics as scalable 3D object detection, neural implicit representations, differentiable rendering, and domain adaptation.

Reviewer for CVPR, ICCV, BMVC, WACV, ICRA, IROS, RA-L, IJCV.
