Sergey Zakharov

I am a Senior Research Scientist at Toyota Research Institute and a former PhD candidate at the Chair for Computer Aided Medical Procedures, Technical University of Munich and Siemens AG. My research focuses on 3D scene understanding and covers such topics as neural implicit representations, generative modeling, scalable 3D object detection, and domain adaptation.

Reviewer for CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, ICRA, IROS, SIGGRAPH.
