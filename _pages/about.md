I am a machine learning research scientist at [Toyota Research Institute](https://www.tri.global/) and a former PhD candidate at the Computer Vision Group at the [Chair for Computer Aided Medical Procedures](http://campar.in.tum.de/WebHome), Technical University of Munich and a Doktorand at Siemens AG. My work research focuses on the development on scalable object detection, pose estimation, differentiable rendering, and domain adaptation. Reviewer for CVPR, ICCV, BMVC, WACV, ICRA, IROS, RA-L, CVIU.
